About Tokyo Luxury Drive

Welcome to our Real Tokyo Drift JDM Experience!

Come with us to have a best experience in Tokyo!
On our tour, you'll get the chance to ride in a Tokyo Drift RX-7 and other iconic JDM cars with local content creators. We will visit popular car meeting spots and scenic photo locations around Tokyo. We will have photo sessions and vedio shooting sessions recreating the movie scene.

Our expert drivers and concierge team ensure an unforgettable journey through Tokyo's most iconic routes.

Our tours are available for both small and large groups!
Unlike other tours, you’ll get to experience the true Tokyo Drift vibes. You’ll visit local spots that only the locals know, while learning about JDM culture and enjoying great conversations.
