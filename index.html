About Us

Welcome to our Real Tokyo Drift JDM Experience!

Come with us to have a best experience in Tokyo!

On our tour, you'll get the chance to ride in a Tokyo Drift RX-7 and other iconic JDM cars with local content creators. We will visit popular car meeting spots and scenic photo locations around Tokyo. We will have photo sessions and video shooting sessions recreating the movie scene.

Note: Veilside RX-7 is only available on SATURDAY & SUNDAY

Our tours are available for both small and large groups!

Unlike other tours, you'll get to experience the true Tokyo Drift vibes. You'll visit local spots that only the locals know, while learning about JDM culture and enjoying great conversations.
